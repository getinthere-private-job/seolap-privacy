Seolap Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Seolap (the "App") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information.

1. Personal Information We Collect

Seolap does not require account creation or sign-up, and does not directly collect personal information. All content (drawers, cards, photos, text, voice recordings, PDFs) is stored only on your device.

In the future, if new features such as account creation, cloud backup, or AI-based services are introduced, additional personal information (e.g., email, name) may be collected. In such cases, this Privacy Policy will be updated prior to collection, and separate user consent will be obtained.

2. Data Storage and Retention

All content created in the App is stored locally on your device (SQLite database). Your data is not transmitted to any server or backed up to any cloud service.

If you delete the App, all data will be permanently removed from your device.

In the future, if a cloud backup feature is introduced, your content may be stored on external servers with your consent. This feature will only be activated by your choice, and encrypted transmission and storage will be applied.

3. Device Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

All permissions are optional. Declining a permission does not affect the use of other features in the App.

4. Location Information

The App does not track your real-time location. It reads GPS metadata (EXIF) embedded in photos to display markers on the map. Location data is processed only on your device and is not transmitted externally.

5. AI Voice Summary

When you use the AI voice summary feature, your recorded audio is sent to the Google Gemini API to generate a text summary.

  • Recipient: Google Gemini 2.5 Flash API
  • Data sent: Voice recording file
  • Purpose: Generate a text summary of the voice content
  • Retention: The summary is stored on your device. Google's data handling is governed by the Google Gemini API Terms of Service.

The AI summary feature only operates when you explicitly request it. Audio data is never sent automatically.

6. Calendar Sync

When you enable the calendar sync feature, the App's to-do data is synchronized with your device's default calendar app.

  • Scope: To-do (Today) data within the App
  • Processing: Handled locally via the device's calendar API (iOS EventKit)
  • External transmission: None — all synchronization occurs on your device

Calendar access is optional. Declining the permission does not affect other features.

7. App Usage Analytics

Seolap uses Google Analytics for Firebase to analyze app usage patterns. Information collected includes:

  • Screen views and events (which features are used)
  • Session information (usage duration, frequency)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)

Collected data is used solely for app improvement purposes and does not include personally identifiable information (name, email, etc.). For more details, see the Google Privacy Policy.

8. Advertising

Seolap uses Google AdMob to display rewarded and native ads. AdMob may collect the following information for ad serving and analytics:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

On iOS 14 and later, the App requests App Tracking Transparency (ATT) consent. If you decline, personalized ads will be limited. For more details, please refer to the Google Privacy Policy.

11. AI Summary Credit Recovery

If an AI voice summary fails due to a server error, the consumed credits are automatically restored. Credits are not restored if the user manually cancels the summary.

12. PDF Sharing

When exporting cards as PDF or sharing as ZIP, the files are generated on your device and you directly choose the recipient. The files do not pass through our servers.

13. Children's Privacy

Seolap is not intended for children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that information from a child under 13 has been collected, we will promptly delete it.

14. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated to improve our services or provide new features. In case of significant changes, we will notify you in advance through in-app popups or push notifications. The revised policy takes effect from the date it is posted.

Users who do not agree with the updated Privacy Policy may continue using the existing version of the App without updating. However, access to new features may be limited without the update.
